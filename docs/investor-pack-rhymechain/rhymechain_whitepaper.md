# RHYMECHAIN WHITE PAPER v1.0
**The Future of Fantasy Music & Web3 Artist Economics**

---

## 1. ABSTRACT
RhymeChain is the first decentralized fantasy league for hip-hop culture. By bridging real-world streaming data with blockchain-based digital assets, we create a "Play-to-Earn" ecosystem where fans act as label executives and artists gain a sustainable revenue model.

## 2. THE MARKET GAP
### 2.1 The Artist Devaluation
The streaming era has democratized distribution but decimated artist income. An artist needs millions of streams to earn a living wage.
### 2.2 The Passive Fan Experience
Current music consumption is passive. Fans have no stake in the success of the artists they discover early.
### 2.3 The Fantasy Sports Success
Sorare and NBA Top Shot have proven that digital scarcity combined with "utility-rich" gaming creates billions in value. Hip-hop is the largest cultural force globally but lacks a dedicated fantasy platform.

## 3. THE ECOSYSTEM
### 3.1 Digital Artist Cards (NFTs)
Artists are represented by unique, tiered NFT cards (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary).
### 3.2 The Oracle System
RhymeChain uses a proprietary Oracle service that pulls streaming data from Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube to determine weekly leaderboard points.
### 3.3 Tournaments ("The Charts")
Users enter their "Lineups" into weekly leagues. Winners receive $RHYCON tokens, merchandise, and rare cards.

## 4. ARTIST ECONOMICS
### 4.1 Primary Sales (80/20 Split)
80% of funds from initial card drops go directly to the artist.
### 4.2 Secondary Market (10% Royalty)
Artists receive a 10% royalty on every peer-to-peer trade of their cards, forever.

## 5. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
### 5.1 Layer 2 Scaling (zkEVM)
To ensure low gas fees and fast transactions, RhymeChain is built on Linea/zkEVM.
### 5.2 Smart Contract Suite
- **Card Minter:** Governance-gated minting.
- **Marketplace:** Non-custodial trading.
- **Tournament Manager:** Data-driven reward distribution.
