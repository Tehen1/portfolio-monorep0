# RHYMECHAIN TOKENOMICS TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION
**Token: $RHYCON (Utility & Governance)**

---

## 1. TOKEN SUMMARY
- **Ticker:** $RHYCON
- **Standard:** ERC-20 (zkEVM)
- **Total Fixed Supply:** 1,000,000,000
- **Decimals:** 18

## 3. CORE UTILITY MECHANISMS
### 3.1 Tournament Entry
Users must pay entry fees in $RHYCON for high-stakes leagues. 20% of these fees are burnt.
### 3.2 Label Staking
Holding $RHYCON allows users to upgrade their "Virtual Label", unlocking higher reward tiers and exclusive artist drops.
### 3.3 Marketplace Currency
Preferred currency for buying/selling cards, offering 0% fees compared to 2.5% when using ETH/USDC.

## 4. MONETARY POLICY
### 4.1 Buy-back and Burn
50% of platform revenue (from Primary Sales and Marketplace fees) is used to buy back $RHYCON from the market and burn it.
### 4.2 Artist Incentives
Artists earn $RHYCON bonuses for high streaming performance, aligning their marketing efforts with platform growth.

## 5. REWARD FORMULAS
The reward $R$ for a tournament is calculated as:
$R = (P \times S) / \Sigma(P \times S)$
Where:
- $P$ is the card's rarity multiplier.
- $S$ is the normalized streaming score.
